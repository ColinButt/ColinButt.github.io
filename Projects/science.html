A serious game designed to teach children how to perform scientific experiments in a safe, virtual environment. In this game, players can light bunsen burners, change the colour of fire, create towers of flame and more!

My roles

I joined this group as the Lead Programmer, and while I had help from the other members, I did most of the coding myself. I also worked with our designers to conceptualise mechanics, levels and puzzles, and discussed concepts and ideas with them. I also spent a lot of time working on the particles, making the different coloured, but still realistic, flames you see in the video.
