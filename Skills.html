My Game Dev Skills!

I've had a long and varied history of education and learning, so to summarise, here's just my history in Game Development!

Games Design

Ever since I was a child, I always had interest in understanding, creating and adapting rules to games and other ideas. I also loved playing games as a kid, as well as reading and listening to discussions about design philosophy, balance So, really, it shouldn't have come as that much of s surprise when I realised my dream in life was to become a Games Designer.

Games Programming

I discovered I loved programming about halfway through my design course when I took my Introduction to Programming class. Perhaps it was my past experience with HTML that helped, or my inherent desire to just make a fun game, but regardless I took to it like a fish to water! I spent the rest of that classing devouring and speeding through tutorials, both in class and out of class, to the point where I managed to mostly teach myself how to program, albeit only in the Unity engine. From there I continued through the course, volenteering for and doing about the programming work I had in any project. This culminated in my major project, where I decided to a team as their only Programmer to create Amor Fati.

Now I'm studying for an Advanced Diploma in Games Programming at the AIE, and am finally learning many theoretical concepts such as object oriented programming and matrix Mathematics.
