Current Projects

While I always ensure I put my maximum effort into my work, I often find myself with more off-time than I'd like, so here's a few things I'm working on.

My most recent project, which I just completed was Kingmaker, a strategy game build in Allegro you can see above.

  • My next priority project is constructing a 1D roguelike using the Monogame framework as an opportunity to brush up on more "Traditional" C# coding.
  • I'm also designing A Philosopher's Family, an RPG where a young alchemist must reconcile with his siblings and father before a mysterious illness claims his life,
  • and Slay No Evil, a game where a Priest must lead a small community of farmers and villagers in a desperate attempt to redeem and protect them
  • Finally, I'm prototyping and polishing Benefactor, an Isometric RPG where independent agents use their emotional manifestations to combat those who cannot control their own.
