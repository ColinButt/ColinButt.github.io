Me as a Game Developer!

My current work focus is Mathtastic Monsters, a game I'm working on in a team as part of my final studies at AIE. In Mathtastic Monsters, players build a monster and use it to fight RPG battles against monsters by answering maths questions. The game is being developed with a mobile focus, and is targeted towards children who can use it as an opportunity to practice up on skills they learn in class
